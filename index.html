Duality of Survival and Morality
duality

Fallout 4, developed by Bethesda Game Studios, immerses players in a post-apocalyptic world where they must navigate the duality of survival and morality. Starting in Vault 111, players emerge into the desolate wasteland of Boston, where every decision can mean the difference between life and death.

Minutemen

The Minutemen are a faction dedicated to protecting and defending the inhabitants of the post-apocalyptic Commonwealth. The organization has its roots in history and is inspired by the militias that fought during the American Revolutionary War in the 18th century.

Brotherhood of Steel

The Brotherhood of Steel is one of the most well-known and influential factions in the Commonwealth. Originally founded in the years immediately following the Great War, this technophile military organization is dedicated to the protection and preservation of technology to prevent its misuse by humanity.

Institut

The Institute is a mysterious and highly advanced organization operating deep underground in the post-apocalyptic Commonwealth. Known for its advanced technology and science, the Institute represents one of the most powerful and controversial factions in the Commonwealth.
